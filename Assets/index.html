    Genetic Tests

    Genes are DNA instructions that we all inherit from our parents. Different mistakes in our genes, called mutations, can cause different diseases. Some of these diseases are rare, like cystic fibrosis or Huntingtons disease, others are common, like cancer. Genetic tests can be used to confirm the diagnosis or give you information about possible development of a disease.

    • PCR
    • DNA Sequencing
    • Cytogenetics (Karyotyping and FISH)
    • Microarrays
