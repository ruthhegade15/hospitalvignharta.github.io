Our Department

Department of Urology

Our urologists specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of common urological conditions, including urinary tract infections, kidney stones, bladder problems, prostate issues, and urinary incontinence. We offer comprehensive evaluations, diagnostic tests, and personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs.

Urological (Genitourinary) Cancers

Urological (genitourinary) cancers refer to the types of cancers that affect the organs and structures of the genitourinary system. The genitourinary system includes the urinary tract (kidneys, bladder, ureters, and urethra) as well as the male reproductive system (prostate, testicles, and penis). Urological cancers can occur in any organs.

Department of Female Urology

The Department of Female Urology is a specialized branch of urology that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of urological conditions specific to female patients. It addresses a range of urological issues that primarily affect women, including conditions related to the urinary tract, pelvic floor, and female reproductive system.
