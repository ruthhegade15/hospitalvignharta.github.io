Dermatology services

We are dedicated to providing exceptional dermatology services to help you achieve healthy and radiant skin. Our team of experienced dermatologists and skincare specialists is committed to delivering personalized care and effective treatments tailored to your unique needs.

Emergancey care

Our dedicated team of emergency medical professionals is committed to delivering high-quality care in critical situations. With our state-of-the-art facility and advanced technology, we are equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies with efficiency and compassion.

Dental care services

We are committed to providing exceptional dental care services to help you achieve optimal oral health and a beautiful smile. Our team of experienced dentists and dental hygienists is dedicated to delivering personalized care and comprehensive treatments tailored to your individual needs

Diagnostic imaging

We offer advanced diagnostic imaging services to aid in the accurate diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions. Our state-of-the-art imaging technology and skilled radiologists ensure that you receive high-quality imaging services in a safe and comfortable environment.

Neurology services

Our dedicated team of neurologists is committed to providing exceptional neurology services to diagnose, treat, and manage a wide range of neurological conditions. With advanced diagnostic tools and a patient-centered approach, we strive to improve the quality of life for individuals experiencing neurological disorders.

Mental health care

We understand the importance of mental health and its impact on overall well-being. Our dedicated team of mental health professionals is committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to support individuals facing mental health challenges.

Hospice care services

We are dedicated to providing compassionate and comprehensive hospice care services to patients and their families during the end-of-life journey. Our interdisciplinary team of healthcare professionals is committed to ensuring comfort, dignity, and emotional support for patients with life-limiting illnesses.

General surgery services

We offer a comprehensive range of general surgery services to address a wide variety of medical conditions. Our team of experienced and skilled general surgeons is dedicated to providing high-quality surgical care using the latest techniques and technology. Whether you require a routine procedure or a complex surgery, we are committed to delivering safe and effective treatments tailored to your individual needs.

Gastroenterology services

Our gastroenterology department is committed to providing exceptional care for patients with gastrointestinal conditions. Our team of highly skilled gastroenterologists, specialized nurses, and support staff work together to deliver comprehensive diagnostic, therapeutic, and preventive services.

Laboratory services

Our state-of-the-art laboratory plays a crucial role in patient care by providing accurate and timely diagnostic services. Our dedicated team of highly trained laboratory professionals, including pathologists, medical technologists, and technicians, work diligently to deliver reliable results and contribute to the overall healthcare experience of our patients.

Nursing services

Our nursing team plays a vital role in providing compassionate and high-quality patient care. Our dedicated nurses are committed to delivering excellent healthcare services and ensuring the well-being of our patients. With their expertise, compassion, and commitment, they form an essential part of our healthcare team.

Oncology services

Our dedicated team of oncologists and multidisciplinary specialists is committed to providing exceptional oncology services to patients fighting cancer. We understand the physical and emotional challenges that come with a cancer diagnosis, and we are here to support you every step of the way. Our comprehensive approach to cancer care combines cutting-edge treatments, advanced technology, and personalized support to deliver the best possible outcomes.

Ophthalmology services

Our ophthalmology department is dedicated to providing comprehensive eye care services to patients of all ages. Our team of experienced ophthalmologists and eye care professionals is committed to preserving and improving vision health. We offer a wide range of ophthalmology services, from routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures, ensuring that all your eye care needs are met under one roof.

Orthopaedics services

Our Orthopaedic department is dedicated to providing exceptional musculoskeletal care to patients of all ages. Our team of skilled orthopaedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and support staff is committed to delivering comprehensive and personalized treatment plans for a wide range of orthopaedic conditions.

Outpatient services

We understand that not all medical conditions require inpatient care. That's why we offer a comprehensive range of outpatient services, allowing patients to receive the care they need while maintaining the comfort of their own homes. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing high-quality outpatient services in a convenient and patient-centered environment.

Paediatrics services

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate pediatric care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of experienced pediatricians and healthcare professionals understands the unique healthcare needs of children and strives to deliver the highest quality of care to promote their health and well-being.

Psychiatry services

Our psychiatry department is committed to providing comprehensive mental health care services to individuals of all ages. We understand the impact of mental health on overall well-being and strive to deliver compassionate and personalized care to our patients. Our team of experienced psychiatrists, psychologists, and mental health professionals is dedicated to helping individuals overcome mental health challenges.

Psychology services

Our psychology department is dedicated to providing comprehensive mental health services to individuals seeking support and guidance. Our team of experienced psychologists and mental health professionals is committed to helping individuals overcome challenges, improve their mental well-being, and enhance their overall quality of life.

Pharmacy services

Our pharmacy plays a crucial role in providing safe and effective medication management for our patients. Our dedicated team of pharmacists and pharmacy technicians is committed to ensuring that patients receive the highest quality of pharmaceutical care. We strive to optimize medication therapy, promote patient safety, and improve health outcomes.

X-ray & radiology
